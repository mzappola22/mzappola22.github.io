Myles Zappola's Blog

by Myles Zappola

Posted on March 9th, 2017 at 9:00 AM

Monday Feb 26, 2018: Today in Innovation lab, we got to use the Oculus Rift and Samsung VR. The virtual reality experience was amazing and felt so real but after a while made me dizzy. I then used the Oculus Rift to paint with Tilt brush. With the Tilt Brush app, You can paint anything. The painting on this app and the painting in reallife are very different. Painting in Tilt Brush is 3D and has many more features that make it easy to paint and erase. After playing with the VR, we played with a 360 degree camera. We got to make fun videos with it. When we all used the camera we immideatley noticed something. The lens for it looks like a fish eye lens and can see everything near it. We could put our videos into VR's and see everything in the area. The three VR headset's that we used were the Google Cardboards, Gear VR's, and the Oculus Rift. The Cardboard is a basic VR that you put your phone in. The Gear VR is similar to that but strictly for Samsung phones which are built to do VR. The Oculus Rift is a very advanced VR that is connected to your computer. The Oculus has many games that you can use it for and also has the ability to use your hands while you are playing. VR is just developing and isn't perfect yet. In the near future I would expect the quality of the VR and the ability to fit it on your head will improve. VR's will probably get smaller and maybe become accessories to everyday life.
